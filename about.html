About CEMS

Mission and Identity

Emergency medical readiness with disciplined execution.

Cadet Emergency Medical Services supports campus operations through trained members, structured leadership, and a professional service mindset. The organization exists to improve readiness, support events, and build capable future leaders in emergency response environments.

What We Do

Event support and operational standby

CEMS helps provide organized medical standby coverage and coordination support for campus activities, training events, and public-facing functions that need a prepared team.

How We Operate

Training, standards, and accountability

Members operate within a defined chain of leadership, recurring training cycles, and shared expectations for communication, readiness, and professional conduct.
